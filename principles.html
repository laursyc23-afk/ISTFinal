Principles of Design

  1. Contrast
      2. Shows large differences between objects or elements
  2. Emphasis
      3. The part that stands out and gets your attention. The "star" of the show!
  3. Rhythym and Movement
      4. Gives your eye a path to follow within the artwork.
  4. Pattern and Repetition
      5. Repeating shapes colors or lines can create a rhythm or pattern.
  5. Variety
      6. Using different shapes, sizes, and colors to keep the artwork interesting.
  6. Proportion
      7. How elements compare to each other.
  7. Balance
      8. Making sure things are organized and evenly spread out on a picture.
  8. Unity
      9. Making sure all parts work together like a team.
